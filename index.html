Education is not just about getting a degree, thus, it does not stop outside the classroom. At BINTAR, students are encouraged to grow in their character & talents in a campus community that aligns with their interests & calling.

In providing holistic education, we ensure that students can learn, practice and live well by providing excellent facilities.

Our most valuable asset is Our Team. We believe that the right people need to be in the right job according to their passion, purpose, and calling. Our lecturers are carefully selected, namely people who are not only competent, but also have the heart to educate future leaders. Here, we work together and grow together as one family.

Carrying out the vision of BINTAR 2035, “a World-class university, fostering and empowering the society in building and serving the nation” BINTAR continues to contribute to fostering and empowering the society to build a better world.
